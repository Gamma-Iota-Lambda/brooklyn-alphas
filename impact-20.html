Impact $20

Did you know that $20 can help expand educational access, mentorship, and community programs in Brooklyn?

Let's Educate The Community Together

Education isn't just about classrooms—it's about community. Every donation directly supports our One GIL vision and our ongoing mission to Educate the Community through programs that inform, empower, and inspire.

Developing academic excellence and leadership in young men through mentorship, workshops, and real-world experiences.

Hosting forums and learning experiences that address financial literacy, civic engagement, health, and social justice.

Expanding access to higher education through the Myles A. Paige Scholarship and new academic partnerships.

Why Impact $20

Brooklyn is home to nearly 2.7 million people, over 1 million households, and hundreds of thousands of families across neighborhoods like East New York, Brownsville, and Canarsie.
