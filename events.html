Chapter Anniversary

Gamma Iota Lambda 80th Anniversary Celebration

May 5, 2025 · Center for Brooklyn History · Brooklyn, NY

The brothers of Gamma Iota Lambda proudly celebrated 80 years of service to the communities of Brooklyn and Long Island. The evening opened with a presentation from Brooklyn History Special Collections Cataloger Deborah Tint, showcasing treasures from our newly processed chapter archives. A thought-provoking panel followed featuring Lurie Daniel Favors, Esq., Dr. Walter M. Kimbrough, Maya E. Latimer, and Dr. André McKenzie — honoring our past and propelling our purpose forward. For eight decades, we've lived our mission: "Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. develops leaders, promotes brotherhood and academic excellence, while providing service and advocacy for our communities."
